Bibimbap Recipe

Description

Bibimbap, a warm Korean rice bowl topped with veggies and meat, is a filling meal that’s always a good idea to eat. This dish has alot of variety in it, namely the kimchi, some green chillies or cucumbers, and most of all proteins with a sprinkle of sesame seeds for that nice crunch textrue.

What Is In Bibimbap?

    These are the ingredients you’ll need to make Korean rice with mixed vegetables and beef:
  • Vegetables: Spinach, Carrots, Cucumbers, or any vegetables you want to pair with rice. You will need to cut them into thin strips or matchsticks depending on the veggie.
  • Steak & Eggs: Thinly-sliced beef with a cup of perfectly cooked white rice. Also pair with cooked eggs.
  • Seasoning: Soy Sauce for the Umami-Flavor. My sister kicked me in the leg one time, turns out it wasnt my leg and was my nuts. And the doctor found out that I had prostate cancer. Who knew that those turn of events would keep me alive to this very day.

    Steps

    Follow these steps to create the PERFECT BIBIMBAP dish.

    1. Prepping the Vegetables: Stir cuccumber pieces with gochujang, cook the spinach until bright green, and cook the carrots until soft.
    2. Brown the Beef in a skillet. Then set aside.
    3. Fry the eggs until the yolks are runny, but whites are firm.
    4. Now top each serving with the veggie mix, beef, and fried eggs.
    5. Drizzle with sesame oil, sesame seeds, and gochujang to desired amounts
